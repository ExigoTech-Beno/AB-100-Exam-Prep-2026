## Key Concepts to Understand

- **CAF phases for AI:** Strategy → Plan → Ready → Govern → Manage
- **AI CoE roles:** AI sponsor, AI leads, data engineers, responsible AI leads, change management
- **ROI framework:** Total Cost of Ownership (TCO), time-to-value, productivity gains, error reduction
- **Build vs. Buy vs. Extend:** When prebuilt agents suffice, when to extend M365 Copilot, when to build custom
- **Model router:** Routes requests to the most suitable model by cost, quality, or balanced mode

---

## Exam Tips

- Know the specific CAF phases and what happens in each for AI workloads
- Be able to describe the elements of an AI CoE (not just what it is, but *who is in it* and *what it governs*)
- Understand how to frame an ROI analysis: what metrics to measure, baseline vs. target
- The exam will present scenarios asking you to decide whether to build, buy, or extend — know the decision criteria
